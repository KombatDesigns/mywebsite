What is Future Finance MD?+

An independent, physician-run hub delivering unbiased money education – no ads, no hidden commissions.

Is the content Canada-only?+

No. Core strategies are universal; dedicated regional notes (US, UK, AUS, GCC) sit under International Guidance.

Can you manage my portfolio?+

We do not manage assets. Instead, we help you vet fiduciary planners and understand their recommendations.

About the Author

Dr S. Elfirjani, an Ontario-based physician, paid off six-figure med-school debt and discovered a passion for personal finance along the way. After completing advanced coursework in tax & planning, he created Future Finance MD to demystify money for colleagues worldwide.

He partners with CFPs, CPAs, and lawyers to keep advice accurate and product-agnostic. Outside clinic hours he’s a die-hard trail-runner, espresso nerd, and dad of two.
