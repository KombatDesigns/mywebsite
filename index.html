Frequently Asked Questions

Is this site only for physicians?

No. While many examples mention doctors, the frameworks apply to nurses, pharmacists, allied-health professionals, med-students, and anyone working in the health ecosystem.

Do I need to incorporate as soon as I graduate?

Usually not. Incorporation pays off once your net income comfortably exceeds living costs—otherwise the admin fees may outweigh tax savings. A good accountant can model break-even points for your specialty and province/state.

Where should I start if I’m overwhelmed?

Begin with cash-flow: track spending for one month, build an emergency fund, and automate savings into tax-advantaged accounts. Small wins compound; you can layer billing, investing, and tax strategies later.
